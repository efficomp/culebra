genotype module¶

This module defines genotypes (genetic codings) for the different kinds of individuals involved in feature selection problems. The genotype of one individual is basically defined by the Individual subclass it belongs to, and also by the Species constraining all the individuals of that class.

By the moment, two genotypes are defined:

  • The feature_selection sub-module defines individuals and species designed to select the most relevant features of a dataset

  • The classifier_optimization sub-module defines individuals and species to support the co-evolution of the classifier used within the Wrapper while the better features are also being selected
